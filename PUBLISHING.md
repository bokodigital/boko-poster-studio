# Publishing routing — Boko Content Command Center

When asked to **post approved posts**, publish each to the channel below.
Always confirm before any live, public post.

| Platform  | Account / destination                      |
|-----------|--------------------------------------------|
| Facebook  | **Boko Digital Solutions** page            |
| Instagram | **Boko Digital Solutions** account         |
| LinkedIn  | **Maria's profile** (Maria Jose Mendieta)  |
| YouTube   | **Boko Digital Solutions** channel         |
| Hubspot   | **Boko Digital** account (email)           |

Notes:
- Only posts with status **Approved** are eligible to publish.
- A post's **Platform** field on the board determines the destination.
- Use the **Publish / Schedule** button on the board: pick a type (All / Posts /
  Reels / Carousels / Emails), and for each Approved item it opens the right
  destination (per the table above), copies the caption/email, and downloads the
  image so you paste & schedule in-platform. You confirm every live send.
- Tools opened per platform: Instagram/Facebook → Meta Business Suite
  (Boko Digital Solutions), LinkedIn → LinkedIn share (Maria), YouTube →
  YouTube Studio, Hubspot → HubSpot email (Boko Digital).
